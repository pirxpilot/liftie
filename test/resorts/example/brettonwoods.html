    • Bretton Woods Morning Snow Report for Saturday, December 27, 2025

      A Magical Winter Reset! Those on the mountain Friday were treated to a rare and beautiful “sundog”—a stunning halo of light and ice crystals in the sky. It was a spectacular backdrop for a day where the snow felt ‘plush with a bite,’ offering incredible grip and smooth turns. Thanks to a huge push from our Ski Patrol yesterday, we have officially expanded our terrain count to accommodate the holiday crowds.

      Operational Summary: We move into the heart of the holiday weekend with 7 lifts spinning from 8:00 AM until 4:00 PM, including the West Mountain Express! —Note: West Mountain Quad, Rosebrook Quad, and the Red Carpet will close at 3:30 PM, in preparation of mountain closing efforts from patrol. Today’s weather looks perfect for the slopes: partly to mostly sunny with temperatures in the teens and low winds. As of Saturday morning, we will have a total of 51 trails and 8 glades open on a machine groomed/packed powder surface.

      Terrain Highlights: With West Mountain now fully integrated into our daily operations, you can enjoy freshly groomed corduroy on Joseph’s Run, Little Tuckerman’s, and Oscar Barron’s. Patrol has been hard at work dropping ropes on additional stashes off the West Mountain Express, so keep your eyes peeled for new terrain openings throughout the day.

      Mountain Notes: Our snowmaking team is back in action today, focusing on Alpine Garden and Starr King to further increase our trail count. With temperatures remaining in the teens, the snow will stay fast and firm—ideal for those looking to carve. Please remember to respect all “Closed” signs as patrol continues to mitigate hazards in the woods and ungroomed areas. Please

      Plan Your Day: Saturday is expected to be a busy day at the resort. To ensure the fastest path to the snow and the guaranteed lowest rates, please book your lift tickets, lessons, and rentals online in advance. Dress in layers to stay comfortable in the teen-degree weather, and we’ll see you at the summit for another beautiful day at The Woods!

    • For today, Saturday December 27, expect partly to mostly sunny skies during the day and fair conditions at night. Temperatures at the mountain base will range from a morning low of 8°F to a maximum of 20°F mid-afternoon, while the summit will stay between 10°F and 24°F. Winds at the base will remain light from the WNW at 5–12 mph, though summit winds will be more active, shifting from NE to NW at 5–20 mph.

    • Bretton Woods Forecasted Nordic Snow Report for Saturday December 27, 2025

      TOTAL  KM SKATE GROOMED — 16.2km | TOTAL KM TRACK SET — 11.1km

      It’s the holiday weekend, and we have been set up beautifully for some wonderful Nordic skiing with bright sun and silky snow.

      Trails groomed today without track are Dark Forest, Beech Hill, Clinton-D, Lower Fairway, Crawford Hollow, B&M, Porcupine, & Sebosis.

      Groomed today with track, we have Perimeter, Zealand, Turnpike, Brookside, Chris’s Crossing, Outlook, Esker, Bridle, Links, & Mt Pleasant, all others have been groomed within the last 24 hours. We are continuing to check trails and look forward to opening additional terrain whenever we can.

      Trails can be accessed from the Nordic Center.  Please use caution while skiing as there are still a few thin spots, grass, ice & open water spots along the way.

      We hope you enjoy some of our new snowfall and trails groomed to perfection. Happy Holidays!

    • For today, Saturday December 27, expect partly to mostly sunny skies during the day and fair conditions at night. Temperatures at the mountain base will range from a morning low of 8°F to a maximum of 20°F mid-afternoon, while the summit will stay between 10°F and 24°F. Winds at the Nordic Center will remain light from the WNW at 5–12 mph.
