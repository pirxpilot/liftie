  • For on hill temperatures and wind readings ( at 9,800 ft) visit our Data Site at the top of Dreamcatcher Chairlift.

*Grand Targhee Resort's main lifts Dreamcatcher and Shoshone operate from 9:00am - 4:00pm. The Sacajawea & BLackfoot lifts operate from 9:00am - 3:30pm. All snowfall is estimated using instrumentation and data from our Chief Joe Bowl site(elevation 8,800 ft) at 5:00am. Additional accumulations between 5:00am and lift opening, on heavy snow days, will also be displayed above. This amount is then included in the next day's 24 hour snowfall total. You may also experience greater depths at higher elevations or on different terrain aspects. Physical measurements are recorded during the day with season totals adjusted accordingly.
