* Surface Notes: Snow in the forecast, get ready for a great time at Windham Mountain!

February 8, 2013 - Projected

Snow-topped roofs, freshly powdered trails in the distance a picturesque day at Windham Mountain is only a snow storm away! We will groom all of our open terrain tonight 41 trails -  so that when the snow fall begins it will be a powder mecca. Get ready for a snow day!

For Friday we will offer top to bottom skiing and riding on 41 trails, all 5 terrain parks and 262 skiable acres serviced by 6 lifts.  The forecast for today calls snow throughout the day with temps in the high 20s at the summit and low 30's at the base. 

We will be turning our lifts from 9:00am - 8:00pm with Day Lift tickets $52 adults, $45 young adults (13-17), $37 juniors (7-12) and 6 and under $10; Flex Tickets (any 4 hours) are $39 adults, $31 young adults (13-17), $24 juniors (7-12) and 6 and under $10; Sunset (2-8pm): $45 adults, $41 young adults (13-17), $37 juniors (7-12); Night (4-8pm): $37 ages 7+.

The Adventure Park is open Fri 5-8pm, Sat 11am-8pm and Sun 11am-4pm with tubing, zip line, XC Skiing, snowshoeing and kids snowmobiles!
