2025-26 Winter Season

Lift Operating Hours: 9am4pm

We have snow set to begin this evening and the pattern should be active for the next few days. Take advantage of the long weekend to get some warm up laps in before Mother Nature reloads the mountain!

Temperatures on Baldy should range from a low of 24 to a high of 30. The valley forecast calls for a partly sunny day, with clouds increasing in the afternoon. Expect precipication to begin around dinner time with snow continuing into tomorrow.

Portions of Greyhawk and Hemingway terrain will be closed mornings and early afternoons February 1727 for Paralympic Training and the Laura Flood Memorial Race.Partial access to race country remains available for spectating and skiing. Other Warm Springs terrain is unaffected.
*Variable conditions may exist when exiting race country terrain.

Uphill Access:

  • Bald Mountain Uphill Access will be open Monday evening, 2/16
    • Bald Mountain Uphill Access will be CLOSED Tuesday morning, 2/17
  • Dollar Mountain Uphill Access is open.

Upcoming Events:

  • Fri. 2/20: Aprs-Ski Live Music at River Run
  • Sat. 2/21: Aprs-Ski Live Music at Warm Springs
  • Fri. 3/6: Dollar Holler Rail Jam
