                              Pistes opening

                              14th of March 2020
                              Following the government’s announcement this evening the Morzine ski area, plus restaurants and bars will be closed as of Sunday 15th March. Please contact us by email on info@morzine-avoriaz.com  with any questions.

                              SEASON 2019-2020
                              The partial opening period of Morzine for the winter season 19/20 is Saturday 14nd of December 2019.
                              Morzine will close on Monday 13th of April 2020 if snow conditions are suficient.

                              The estimated opening date for the Avoriaz area for the 19/20 winter season is Friday 13nd of December 2019.
                              Avoriaz will close on Sunday 19th of April 2020 if snow conditions are sufficient.

                              With around 650 kilometers of slopes to explore, Morzine and the Portes du Soleil is one of the largest connected ski areas in Europe with never ending potential to explore. And if you want to explore the colossal variety of terrain then this winter-paradise has an extensive network of 197 well-connected ski lifts that is open through the winter season.
                               
                              From beautiful easy flowing tree lined runs through to some of Europe’s steepest most challenging unpisted black runs, you’ll find a run to suit. With twelve resorts linked under one pass, make the most of your stay in Morzine by exploring the full ski area.
                               
                              The opening times can change due to snowfall. Keep an eye on the real-time lift status which is updated every day to help you know what’s open where.

                              During the season, info on slopes and ski lifts is constantly updated. Please note, the slopes and lifts will be indicated as closed every evening and until the next morning before the daily reopening.
