Mountain Updates

Friday, October 1, 2021:

Hikers:

Today we’re looking at cold temps, rain/snow mix and snowy trail conditions. Currently, the only hiking on the mountain is the Chair 3 – 7 road. Please dress accordingly, bring layers, proper footwear and use caution, you will encounter snow and mud. The tram is running daily starting at 11 a.m. for sightseeing and access to the upper mountain station and downloads, face masks are required while on the tram.

This week’s hiking terrain MAP

Winner Creek, Winner Creek Extension Trail, and Upper Winner Creek are also in great shape for hiking. Please exercise caution on trails if you see wildlife, it’s always a good to carry bear spray with you and know how to use it.
