Conditions Report

Thursday November 26, 2015 Last update 04:05 p.m.

Storm in progress
Winter Weather Advisory in effect until 4:00 a.m. Wednesday

It was a blustery day at the mountain. We experienced consistent Cat 1 Hurricane force winds at the top of Pine Marten. The nine inches of new snow from the last storm got blown around, but will help set up good skiing and riding conditions for Friday. 

All facilities and services will be closed on Thanksgiving day including the gate leading into West Village. There will be no access into the Mt. Bachelor parking lot on Thanksgiving day. 

Operations will commence on Friday with our official kick off to the 2015-2016 season and the opening of Pine Marten, Skyliner, and Sunrise! It is forecasted to be a beautiful day and will be a great way to work off a hearty Thanksgiving meal.  

Please keep in mind our Uphill Travel Policy is restricted to the Cone while Summit is closed.

**As a reminder snow base depth is limited and early season conditions are in effect. Please ski with caution as there could be unmarked obstacles.**

All 2015-2016 Full Season, Midweek and 12 Day passes along with 4-Packs are valid to use. Early season ticket pricing will be in effect for single day lift tickets. 

 

Snow Stake Cam

Located in the West Village base area. The image updates every 15 minutes, 24 hours a day. We clear the snow from the stake each afternoon at lift-closing time so that you can see how much new snow will be waiting for you in the morning!
