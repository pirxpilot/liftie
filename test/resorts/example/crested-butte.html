    Winter Activities

    The Mountain Concierge in the new Adventure Center at Mountaineer Square can book many valley-wide activities and adventures including Sleigh ride Dinners, Cross country skiing, Guided Snowmobile Tours, Guided Backcountry Ski / Snowboard Tours, Winter Horse Trail ride, Horse drawn carriage rides, Tennis lessons, Dogsled Tours, Spa Services and more.

    Learn more

    Summer Activities

    There's a saying in the mountains that says, "I came for the winter, but I stayed because of the summer." Come summer time, the trails, rivers and peaks surrounding Crested Butte explode with a bevy of activity. Fishing, hiking, mountain biking, kayaking, golfing or just basking in the Colorado sun are just a few of the things that one can enjoy when the snow melts.
    Check out everything we have to offer. Whether it's on the mountain or off, there is sure to be something for everyone.

    Dining

    The adventure in Crested Butte doesn't just stop at the on-mountain experience. The sense of exploration continues off the slopes with some of the most diverse and distinctive bars, restaurants and nightlife found in any ski town. From sushi to sandwiches and everything in between, the choices of great places to dine and variety of food types is amazing. For a small town like this to have three locations for sushi, six great pizza places, and more than thirty bars is hard to believe.

    Overview

    Crested Butte has long been recognized as the last great Colorado Ski Town. Words like authentic, genuine, unpretentious and fun come to mind again and again after visiting this spectacular valley. Group events in Crested Butte are unforgettable because of our unmatched surroundings and outstanding celebrations. It is the perfect setting to disconnect from the stresses of everyday life- and reconnect with family, friends and colleagues.

    Learn more

    Weddings

    Crested Butte's breathtaking landscape offers the perfect backdrop for your special day. Spectacular views combine with unique reception venues, professional catering staff, and first class lodging to help you manage all the details of your wedding.

    Learn more

    Meetings

    Nestled high in the Colorado Rockies, Crested Butte is a refreshing change. You can feel the vitality of this great Colorado ski town as you wander through the quaint, Victorian, national historic district with its choices for fine dining, brew pubs, shopping and nightlife.

    School & Youth Group

    Crested Butte Mountain Resort has been hosting church and youth groups for many years offering a fantastic value, a safe resort and town and a memorable trip. With fantastic properties such as the Plaza Condos, Paradise Condos and the Grand Lodge in conjunction with ample meeting space for nightly meetings CBMR can host the ideal trip for your church or youth group.
