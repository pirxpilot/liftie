Activities Report

Gondola Rides & Craft Show This Weekend!

See you October 9 and 10 – the final weekend of our fall season will feature scenic skyrides, recreational activities, and a craft show!

Our four peaks are illuminated with a colorful autumn showcase, providing prime leaf-peeping opportunities. It’s the perfect weekend to welcome fall and get some fresh Adirondack air. Don’t miss your chance to experience the enchanting foliage on a scenic skyride! Enjoy 14+ miles of hiking and downhill mountain biking trails spanning our beautiful peaks. Try our new Leaf Hunt game or one of our hiking challenges, and plan to head out for a round on our 9-basket Airbender Woods disc golf course.

The new Mountain Master Craft Series meets for the final show of the season this weekend featuring nearly 30 talented artisans spread across the base area. Browse handcrafted items such as original paintings and photography, rustic furniture, unique jewelry and ceramics, all-natural bath & beauty essentials, NY-made food, wines and spirits, and more!

Podcast

Podcast transcript | October 2, 2021
Hey Gore Mountain fans, this is Cindy with your More Gore podcast for October 2-3. We are right around halfway to peak foliage and our Northwoods Gondola skyride is open both days this weekend from 10am to 4pm. Enjoy the vibrant fall colors on this relaxing ride with convenient access to even more breathtaking views from our mountaintop observation deck, stone labyrinth, and interpretive loop. Our gondola skyride is fully accessible making it a perfect fall activity for the entire family to do together. Take our new fall leaf hunt with you on the trails - gather the six different types of colorful fallen leaves from our game card to earn a cool Gore Mountain sticker! Check out other free games and challenges on our website or ask about them when you arrive. Extreme downhill mountain biking trails are available, experienced riders can load their bike right onto the gondola and our family-friendly disc golf course in the base area forest is open too. 21/22 season passes include foliage skyrides and bring-a-friend tickets to share, downhill mountain biking, and a round of disc golf. If you don't already have a pass you can see the options on our Season pass page (online). Wondering what's new for winter 21/22? Crews have been busy installing tower guns and replacing pipe on trails like Echo, The Arena, and Lower Sunway. Meanwhile, Black diamond skiers and riders will find all new entrances to the Lower Steilhang trail, Chatterbox Glades, and The Cirque. If you're curious about employment opportunities we've got a variety of seasonal and year-round positions available. Find details online and plan to attend one of our upcoming job fairs on October 23 or November 13. Plan to visit over the weekend of October 9-10 as well. We're anticipating near peak foliage and we'll have both skyrides open and an awesome array of craft vendors in the base area both days! Please check back next Friday for our final fall weekend activities update. Thanks so much for tuning in- make it a great day!

What's Happening...

Games for the Trails
Our fun hiking challenges and games can add more excitement to your adventures. Check out our new Fall Leaf Hunt activity- collect six unique and colorful fallen leaves listed on the game card to earn a cool Gore Mountain sticker!

Shop & Dine with Us
Visit our retail display in the Guest Services Office – save 40% off select clearance items! Meals and snacks are available at the Open Pit Grille – dine on the sundeck or take it with you on the mountain.

Covid Prevention
Please remember that face coverings are required for everyone when inside our lodges, regardless of vaccination status – be sure to review our requirements in advance of your visit.

Skyride Note: Adult passengers carrying children in their arms, backpacks, or other carrying devices, may ride in gondola cabins but not on chairlifts. See our complete scenic skyride policy. Tickets are available online in advance and in-person on the day of your visit.

Special Notes

What’s New for 21/22?
Completed projects include trail modifications and snowmaking improvements on The Arena and Lower Sunway. We added new energy-efficient tower guns on Echo. Look for a new entrance to the double diamond trail Lower Steilhang as well as Chatterbox Glades and the Cirque Glades (the longest in the East!). We’ve also acquired two new snowcats and made several communications upgrades.

Join Our Team!
Year-round and seasonal positions are available. Complete our EZ Apply inquiry to let us know you’re interested and plan to attend one of our upcoming Job Fairs (Oct. 23 & Nov. 13) to meet supervisors and learn about the responsibilities of each department!

Multi-Week Programs On Sale Now!
Explore our fantastic variety of terrain with consistent coaching and camaraderie with friends while building a strong foundation of skills. Program options are available for kids, teens, and adults. Space is limited so book today!
