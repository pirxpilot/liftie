Snow Report

Last updated 04/01/21 2:56 PM

An inch, just one! That's all we got for the month of March. The Spring weather and temps rolled in quickly, but thanks to the endless string of powder days in January and February, we were able to provide you with nearly 100% open terrain throughout the month. We had many excellent days under the sun including last Saturday, but after the sun and rain's extensive damage to the slopes, we simply do not have enough snow to repair terrain for the weekend. Therefore, we have made the difficult decision to remain closed for the remainder of the 2020/21 winter season. 

A special thank you to our amazing staff and guests for your cooperation and ongoing support through it all. It takes a whole village to create this Bromley culture and because of YOU, the slopes remained the perfect escape for many during this challenging time. Enjoy your spring, and we'll see you this summer!

 

Mountain News:

  • 21/22 Season Passes are on sale NOW! Make sure you buy yours early to take advantage of early bird pricing and score the best deal possible. Plus, if you buy your pass before the end of this season, it's valid until closing day of the 20/21 season! Apart than being able to ski whenever you want, there are loads of other benefits included with a Season Pass. For more information and to purchase yours, visit our Season Pass page.
