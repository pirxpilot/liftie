December 1, 2021

Today we celebrated the sunny first day of December with flurries and blue skies, a beautiful time altogether! Tonight there will be even more snowmaking added to Upper Risky Business, Escapade, Lower Grand Rapids, Lower Down Draft, Sirius, Aludra, and Lights Out.  That-a-Way is being added to our groomed trails list tonight, and you can look forward to Lower Risky Business, Tourist Trap, Outta Here, and Lower Lazy River groomed for your enjoyment tomorrow morning as well. Locke Mountain Triple and South Ridge Express are spinning from 9AM-3:45PM to access these trails with ease.

Please note that Barker Mountain Express is not open yet, although you may access Barker Mountain’s terrain on-foot via Jungle Road from Upper Sunday Punch. South Ridge Food Court is now open with a select menu, so go fuel up between runs open Monday-Friday 8:30AM-4:30PM and Saturday-Sunday 7:30AM-4PM. 

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! All skier services will be based out of Barker Lodge once internet is restored. Season Pass Office is also open at South Ridge Lodge and we will have shuttle service between Barker, South Ridge, and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases. 

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

Santa Sunday is this Weekend!

Start celebrating your holiday cheer with hundreds of Santas shredding down South Ridge! This Sunday, December 5, come see or be one of over 250 skiing Santas. Pre-register with a $20 donation online in order to be a Santa, with all proceeds going to the River Fund. If you choose to be in full Santa-attire to participate in our photoshoot festivities, you will receive an all-day lift ticket. Rather leave the beard at home? Spectators are welcome to South Ridge by 11AM, for the best view of all the Santas dashing down the slopes. Please direct any questions you may have about this event to events@sundayriver.com. As a santa on the slope or spectator at South Ridge, we hope to see you there!

Around the Resort

Currently, dining options at the resort include the Barker Lodge Food Court and Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan, open daily for breakfast and lunch, as well as Camp at the Summit, open for breakfast daily, and serving dinner Thursdays through Tuesdays. Dining at South Ridge Lodge will open for the weekend! For full details, visit the River Guide.

See you on the slopes!

-Team Snow

Introducing: The Jordan 8

The next milestone in our Sunday River 2030 plan is coming to life. Opening winter 2022-2023, the Jordan 8 will be the fastest 8-person lift in North America featuring Doppelmayr D-Line's advanced technology and engineered to improve wind resistance, something we are all familiar with in Jordan Bowl. The Jordan 8 also introduces the exciting concept of what will become the Western Reserve, which includes Jordan Bowl, Oz, and future terrain expansion beyond.
