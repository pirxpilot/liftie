General Comments
We're in "waiting for snow" mode. How can you help us get open faster? Well, it depends on what you do when you want it to snow. Remember when you were in school and you'd do anything in the universe to get the snowflakes to fly? Do that now. Maybe that means praying. Maybe that means dancing. Maybe that means finishing off a plate of Brussels Sprouts or broccoli or beets. Whatever you do to encourage Ma Nature to bring snow this way, do it. Want to hike the resort? That's okay for now, but expect unmarked obstacles and no assistance from patrol. Be careful out there. And smile big. While you're here reading our website, do a little shopping. there are still great deals on 5 and 10 time passes, midweek passes, and our new Southside pass. Have a great day, and thanks for riding Meadows!
