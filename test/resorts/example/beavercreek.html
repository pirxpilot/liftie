Skiway status & grooming report

As a courtesy to our homeowners and guests staying in on-mountain lodging properties, scroll down for a list of skiways that can be utilized to access main mountain trails. Please reference the skiway status to ensure your travel is only through open terrain.

Homeowners or guests found on closed terrain and skiways are subject to a possible loss of pass privileges. Skiways are not calculated in the open area percentage.
