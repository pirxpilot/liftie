Mountain Report

Hello from Granite Peak!

That’s a wrap, folks. We are officially closed for the season and already counting down the days until we’re back at it.

What a winter it’s been! We opened as one of the first hills in state on November 28, hit 100% just before Christmas, and kept it rolling as the last resort spinning in Wisconsin. From early turns to spring laps, pond skim laughs to bluebird days, it’s been one for the books.

128 vibe-filled days later, we’re calling it a season. Time to trade ski boots for lawn chairs … at least for a little while.

Still looking to sneak in a few more turns? Head to Snowriver through 4/12 or Lutsen Mountains daily through 4/12, then weekends into early May.

Huge thanks to our team, our community, and all of you who made this season so special. Have a great summer, and we’ll see you back here before you know it. 🎿
