What a Great Week It’s Been!
100% of the Mountain Open!

NOTICE TO OUR GUESTSdo not use your GPS or IPhone Map app to bring you to the ski area or Tube World and DO NOT use any mapping that includes the words SCENIC VIEW DR. or ROAD NAMESHEMPHILL ROAD OR YELLOW PATCH ROAD. Use the directions below for the quickest routes. These roads are incorrect and a mapping error that takes you up a dead end private road and single lane fire roads.

Tube World is not located at the ski area. Tube World is located on U.S. 19 (4721 Soco Road) directly across from the ski area entrance sign atFie Top Road in Maggie Valley.

It’s cold in our neighborhood and please remember to dress appropriately!Today we are skiing on 100% of the mountain on 18 trails. We havethree aerial lifts andtwo conveyor lifts operating with skiing from the top of the mountaindown.Base is 32-66″ and we have a machine worked surface.

We offer three sessions of skiing, day skiing from 8:30am-4:30pm, twilight skiing from 1pm until 10pm and night skiing from 6pm until 10pmwith a morning or afternoon half day rate also available. Weekday rates are in effect and all passes are valid today.

Tube World, located in Maggie Valley, four miles from the ski area across from Ghost Town in the Sky will be open from 10am until 10pm with sessions at 10, 12, 2, 4, 6, and 8pm. Cost for tubing is $25 and you must be 42″ tall to ride alone, there is no two person tubing at Tube World. Wee Bowl, our children’s snowplay area for kids under the 42″ height requirement runs on the same schedule as Tube World and cost for Wee Bowl is $5 per person.Tube World address is 4721 Soco Road.

Our rental shop in Maggie Valley, Cataloochee Ski & Sports,isopen as well. Be sure to stop there for discounted rentals and any accessories you might need prior to your day on the slopes. They are located at 3434 Soco Road.

We remind you to be aware of changing ski conditions and use caution. Natural and manmade obstacles exist and snowmaking and grooming activities are routinely in progress. Please use control and ski or ride only on designated slopes and trails. Dont forget to join us on Facebook and Twitter and or visit www.kidsskifree.com for more information on our Kids Stay and Ski Free program. We here at Cataloochee wish each of you a very safe and happy holiday season and look forward to seeing you on the slopes of Cataloochee this season.
