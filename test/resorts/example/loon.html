AFTERNOON UPDATE 02.22.2021

We saw heavy bands of snow come into the area around lunchtime today and they delivered some goods for afternoon laps. 1” of snow has fallen so far as of around 2pm and it is still coming down. We expect a couple more inches to fall before it is all said and done and it should make for some great laps tomorrow.

Tomorrow you can expect 8 lifts on the roster, all beginning to spin at 9am including the Seven Brothers Triple which will be running all week. Our grooming team will head out after hours and clean up the new snow, and groomers will be awaiting any additional snowfall we receive overnight. Conditions tomorrow will be soft and what better way to start a Tuesday then with some buttery turns first thing in the morning.

Our parks are in full swing as all of them our now open top to bottom. Pick from 6 parks, with countless features, of all different skill levels and stomp that first trick.
