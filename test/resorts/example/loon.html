December 1 2021 | Afternoon Update
We kicked off December in snow showers and by lunch the blue skies were peeking out from behind the clouds. Temps will drop tonight with expected snow flurries later in the evening. Thursday is forecast to be slightly warmer in the mid 30’s with some possible mixed precipitation and light wind in the AM. Join us Thursday at 9am and hop on the Gondola for top to bottom skiing and riding across 64 acres.

After getting some new trails open on Wednesday Loon snowmakers are taking a brief hiatus and will be returning on Friday. Watch this space for updates on new terrain.

The Parks crew is gearing up for the first event of the year, Cease & Desist is back! Registration must be completed in advance, there will be no registration on the day. Learn more Here .

Be aware that at this time the open terrain is suitable for intermediate and advanced level skiers and riders only.

Know Before You Go
COVID-19 protocols, including mask usage indoors, and outdoors when physical distancing cannot be maintained are still in place around the resort.

Welcome back to base lodges but please, leave the large bags at home. Store small backpacks in the cubbies provided.

Learn more by reading the Know Before You Go page.

Pass Holders
Is your pass ready? Login to arrival.loonmtn.com to make sure you have completed your waiver.

Uphill Access
There is no uphill access at this time. Read more here.
