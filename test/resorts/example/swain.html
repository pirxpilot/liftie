Stay & play at Swain during any season

Set in the heart of the Genesee Valley, the Mountainside Inn & Condos at Swain Resort invites skiers and travelers to enjoy a welcome respite after any adventure. Journey along the Finger Lakes Trail, take in the beauty of the “Grand Canyon of the East” at Letchworth State Park, or hike to a 45 ft waterfall at Stony Brook. And of course, don’t forget to plan your winter excursion at Swain.
