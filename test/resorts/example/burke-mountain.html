Mountain Conditions

Weather, Lift & Trail Status, and Webcams

Monday, December 8th

Waking up to 3 inches of fresh snow on a Monday? Suddenly the coffee tastes a little better.
We’re rolling with 4 upper-mountain trails, all accessed via the Mid Burke Express, spinning 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
An early burst of isolated snow showers should taper off before 7 AM, giving way to mostly cloudy skies. Expect a BRISK high near 12°, with a wind-chill dipping to around –7. Bundle up!

Meanwhile, the lower mountain continues to buzz with snowmaking.

Note: The Mid Burke Express services intermediate and advanced terrain only! Upper section of Graham Slam is ungroomed. Lower Warren’s Way will be open until 12 PM. Upper Warren's way is closed with active grooming. Rentals and lessons will be coming online soon.
