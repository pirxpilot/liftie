Daily Mountain Report

Tips Up

The first snow of the season has already coated the peaks, with snow seen in the base area even. See the October Snowfall images on the News Blog. All departments are busy gearing up for the upcoming ski season and we’ve got some exciting improvements in store. Crews are finishing construction on Chair 6 and Quicksilver with load testing scheduled to happen sometime in mid November.

Join us November 14-16 at the Seattle Ski & Snowboard Show where we’ll have great deals onticket packages, stickers, posters and high-fives for everyone. It won’t be long before the mountain is blanketed in white so keep up those snow dances and we’ll see you soon!
