Lift and
Terrain Status

Alta's Mountain Operations keeps this page up-to-date so you can score the freshest laid groomed corduroy to the latest rope drop.

Alta is open with Collins, Sugarloaf, Sunnyside and Wildcat.

Lift, grooming and
trail Status

Mountain environments change daily. Stay in the know with these latest mountain updates including run and grooming status.

In an effort to keep Alta skiers skiing, rather than waiting in lift lines, we will be loading our lifts at full capacity this winter. Singles lines will also return this winter, with the intent that singles join other riders rather than riding solo on the chair. While we are fortunate to enjoy a sport in the wide-open mountain air, facemasks and buffs are still encouraged in lift lines.
