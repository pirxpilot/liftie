LIFTS AND LOTS

Update: We are open for skiing and riding April 7-12 (weather permitting) on the Northwest-base and lift serviced trails only.

Welcome to West Mountain! West Mountain has six lifts: three chairlifts, two surface lifts, and one Rope Tow. We have two parking lots: The Main Base Parking lot that services The Face, West Express Learning Area, and Tubing Area lifts, and the NW Parking Lot that services the Apex chairlift.

WINTER TRAILS AND CONDITIONS
