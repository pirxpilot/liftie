Downhill Area Update


11AM: The Downhill Area is open today from 9am - 10pm. All lifts are scheduled for operation!

The Guest Services office is open 9am - 10pm, 7 days a week.

Pre-booking your tickets online is required and we’ve installed new “Ticket Pickup Boxes” to make it even easier to enjoy your winter experience (you just need to scan the QR code on your confirmation email and your ticket will print automatically). All tickets and season passes can be issued from these new "Pickup Boxes".

Please make sure you review our operational plan https://cypressmountain.com/latest-news/winter-2021-22-plan, so that you are fully prepared for your visit. It is important that we all work together and follow the mandatory COVID-19 protocols so that we can enjoy a fantastic winter season.

For the overnight snowfall depths, see the snow stake cam above.

Terrain Park Report


Gnarly's Den is open! This season Gnarly's has 4 features, which include 1 small box top, 1 dance floor box, 1 extra small box and 1 small table top jump. Skate Park has 1 small floating box, 1 medium jump, 1 medium transfer box, 1 medium pole jam, and 1 medium tubby pyramid.

Snowmaking


We have made lots of snow this season! Our first fire up of the state of the art snowmaking system was on Nov 15th! We will continue to make snow every time the weather permits.

Upcoming Events


Unfortunately, events have been cancelled for the 2021/22 winter season. We hope to go back to normal for the following season.
